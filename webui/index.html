!
PS3 FAT has one physical fan but two thermal zones. The Syscon stores two separate 5-phase tables (CELL / RSX) — the chip with the higher temperature drives the fan. Speeds are 0–255 (≈%·2.55). Below TMin a phase deactivates, above TMax it activates the next. Adjacent phases overlap to create hysteresis.

Checksum Gate (0x3292) Professional Use
!
Newly discovered, not 100% tested. Disabling writes 0xFF to 0x3292, suppressing triple-beep on EEPROM checksum mismatch. This permits CXR -> CXRF transition without an explicit checksum correction step. Only use this if you know what you are doing.

NostaDiag
Welcome to NostaDiag v2.0
A diagnostic workbench for PS3 Syscon chips.

Read error logs, patch RSX chips, inspect EEPROM memory, tune fan curves, apply undervolts — all over UART, directly from your PC. No soldering of a modchip required.
Connect
Step 1 — Connect your PS3
Select your COM port and Syscon type in the top bar, then hit Authenticate.

Not sure which type?
CXR / SW → 57600 baud  ·  CXRF → 115200 baud

The terminal on the right shows all live output. Use / to focus it and type commands directly.
